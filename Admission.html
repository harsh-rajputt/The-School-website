Admissions Now Open

Welcome to Holy Kids School! We are delighted to announce that admissions are now open for the upcoming academic year. We invite parents and guardians to apply for admission for their children in the following grades:

  • Pre-Nursery
  • Nursery
  • Kindergarten
  • Grade 1 to Grade 12

Documents Required for Admission:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Report Card of the last grade attended
  • Passport-sized photographs of the student
  • Parent's ID proof

Admission Process:

  • Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  • Step 2: Submit the required documents.
  • Step 3: Attend the interview or entrance test (if applicable).
  • Step 4: Receive the admission offer letter.
  • Step 5: Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.
